### Key validation rules implemented:

- **ASIN**: Must be exactly 10 uppercase alphanumeric characters
- **Quantity**: Must be between 1 and 999
- **Order ID**: Format XXX-XXXXXXX-XXXXXXX (digits only with dashes)
- **Subscription ID**: Format SXX-XXXXXXX-XXXXXXX (S prefix + 2 digits + dashes)
- **Price Range**: Non-negative, min <= max, both under $999,999.99
- **Return Reasons**: Must be one of 6 valid values (defective, wrong_item, etc.)
- **Frequency**: Between 1 and 26 weeks
- **Search Query**: Not empty, maximum 500 characters
